Our Story

Your Neighbor on Acheh Street

隣 (Tonari) means "neighbor" in Japanese. Originally known as Place We Meet, we rebranded and moved to Acheh Street in the heart of Georgetown's UNESCO heritage zone — bringing the authentic izakaya experience to Penang.

Every skewer is hand-prepared and grilled over charcoal fire, honoring the traditional art of kushiyaki. From chicken and pork skewers to fresh seafood and sushi, our menu spans the best of Japanese comfort food — all at honest prices.

From after-work drinks to weekend celebrations, Tonari is your go-to spot for authentic Japanese comfort food. We also welcome private events and group bookings — reach out to plan your gathering.
