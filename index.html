Our Story

A Place Where People Gather

隣 (Tonari) means "neighbor" in Japanese — a reflection of the warm, communal spirit at the heart of our restaurant. We bring the authentic izakaya experience to Georgetown's historic Acheh Street.

Every skewer is carefully selected and grilled over binchotan charcoal, honoring the traditional art of kushiyaki. Our menu celebrates the simple beauty of quality ingredients, expertly prepared.
